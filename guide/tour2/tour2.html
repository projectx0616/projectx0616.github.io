Guided Tour of Ahmedabad

Day 1: Exploring Historical Ahmedabad

  • Sabarmati Ashram: Start your day by visiting the Sabarmati Ashram, also known as Gandhi Ashram.
  • Sidi Saiyyed Mosque: Explore the exquisite Sidi Saiyyed Mosque, famous for its intricate stone lattice work (jaali).
  • Jama Masjid: Visit one of the most splendid mosques in India, Jama Masjid.
  • Ahmedabad Heritage Walk: Take a guided heritage walk through the old city.
  • Manek Chowk: End your day with a visit to Manek Chowk, a bustling square in the heart of the old city.

Day 2: Modern Ahmedabad and Cultural Delights

  • Kankaria Lake: Start your day with a visit to Kankaria Lake, a picturesque man-made lake.
  • Science City: Explore the fascinating world of science at Gujarat Science City.
  • Adalaj Stepwell: Discover the architectural marvel of Adalaj Stepwell.
  • Calico Museum of Textiles: Dive into Gujarat's rich textile heritage at the Calico Museum.
  • Law Garden Night Market: End your day with a visit to Law Garden Night Market, a lively street market.

Day 3: Day Trips and Nature Escapes

  • Sarkhej Roza: Take a day trip to Sarkhej Roza, a complex of tombs and mosques.
  • Rann of Kutch: If you have more time, embark on a day trip to the Rann of Kutch.
  • Vastrapur Lake: Relax and unwind at Vastrapur Lake, a serene urban oasis.
  • Thol Bird Sanctuary: Birdwatching enthusiasts can visit Thol Bird Sanctuary.
  • Nalsarovar Lake: Another option for nature lovers is Nalsarovar Lake.

This tour offers just a glimpse of what Ahmedabad has to offer. Enjoy your time exploring this vibrant city!
